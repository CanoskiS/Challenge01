MacBookAir

The MacBook Air is a line of Macintosh subnotebook computers developed and manufactured by Apple Inc. It consists of a full-size keyboard, a machined aluminum case, and a thin light structure. The Air is available with a screen size of (measured diagonally) 12.2in (22.782 cm), with different specifications produced by Apple. Since 2011, all MacBook Air models have used solid-state drive storage and intel Core i5 or i7 CPUs.
